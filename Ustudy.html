Physical Health

Physical health is the state of being free from illness or injury. It can cover a wide range of areas including healthy diet, healthy weight, dental health, personal hygiene and sleep. Physical health is vital for overall well-being.

Biological Health

Biological health hazards include bacteria, viruses, parasites and moulds or fungi. ... We provide expertise and resources on monitoring and controlling biological hazards that are transmitted through food, air or water.

Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.
